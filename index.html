// Threat Intelligence
Common UPI Fraud Patterns

UPI Rakshak detects all major fraud vectors active in India's digital payment ecosystem.

🕵️

Mule Account Rings

Stolen money bounces through 10–50 fake accounts to obscure origin before final withdrawal.

⬤ CRITICAL RISK
📞

Vishing / OTP Scams

Caller poses as bank/NPCI official, tricks victim into sharing OTP or approving a collect request.

⬤ HIGH RISK
🔗

Phishing QR Codes

Malicious QR triggers a payment debit instead of receive. Scanning drains your account instantly.

⬤ HIGH RISK
📱

SIM Swap Fraud

Fraudster ports your number, receives all OTPs, and drains your linked bank account.

⬤ CRITICAL RISK
💰

Fake Merchant QR

Counterfeit QR stickers placed over real merchant codes. Payment goes to fraudster, not shop.

⬤ MEDIUM RISK
🤖

Burst Micro-Attacks

Bots test stolen credentials with ₹1–₹9 transfers before executing large withdrawals.

⬤ HIGH RISK
// Protection Guide
Stay Safe from UPI Fraud

9 RBI-recommended rules to protect your UPI accounts and digital wallet.

01

Never Share OTP or PIN

Your bank will NEVER ask for OTP, UPI PIN, or CVV over call. Hang up immediately.

02

Verify QR Before Scanning

Check for stickers placed over QR. Manually type UPI ID for large transactions.

03

Set Daily UPI Limits

In your UPI app settings, cap daily limit to ₹10K–₹25K to limit damage.

04

Enable Transaction Alerts

Real-time SMS + email alerts for every transaction = instant fraud detection.

05

Collect Request = Sending Money

Approving collect sends money OUT. Never approve unknown collect requests.

06

Use Biometric App Lock

Enable fingerprint/face on UPI app. Never store PIN in notes or messages.

07

Verify Recipient Name

Always confirm the name shown by the UPI app matches who you're paying.

08

Report Fraud in 24 Hours

Call 1930 (Cyber Crime Helpline). Early reporting maximises fund recovery.

09

Revoke Unnecessary App Permissions

Remove SMS/call permissions from apps that don't need them.
